About the Artist

Nona Dronova

Contemporary Artist · Doctor of Science · Professor of Gemmology · Art Theorist · Designer

Back to Home

Nona Dronova is a contemporary artist whose multidisciplinary practice combines painting, design, artistic research and art theory. Working at the intersection of Post-Symbolism, Expressionism and Botanical Abstraction, she has developed a distinctive visual language based on colour, texture, rhythm and the emotional transformation of nature.

Alongside her artistic career, Nona Dronova is a Doctor of Science and Professor of Gemmology, internationally recognised for her research into the scientific examination of gemstones, works of art and decorative materials. Her interdisciplinary background has shaped a unique approach in which analytical thinking and artistic intuition exist in harmony, enriching both her creative practice and theoretical research.

For many years she served as Creative Director of one of the largest jewellery holding companies in the Russian Federation, where she led the development of jewellery collections, design concepts and creative strategies. This experience profoundly influenced her understanding of composition, ornament, colour and material culture, which continue to play a significant role in her artistic work.

Her creative practice extends across a wide range of disciplines, including painting, monumental decorative panels, botanical abstraction, lyrical landscapes, diptychs, triptychs, textile art, woven collages, mixed-media compositions, interior art concepts and designer silver jewellery. Each discipline complements the others, forming a coherent artistic universe that transcends the traditional boundaries of painting.

At the core of Nona Dronova's work is the exploration of the dialogue between nature, memory and contemporary visual culture. Rather than reproducing the visible world, she transforms natural forms into expressive compositions where colour becomes the principal language of emotion and visual harmony.

Alongside her studio practice, Nona Dronova develops theoretical research devoted to contemporary painting and artistic language. Her publications explore colour, composition, symbolism, decorative expression and the evolution of modern visual culture, while also presenting her own artistic methodology. These theoretical studies accompany and enrich her creative work, establishing a continuous dialogue between artistic practice and critical reflection.

Her research papers and theoretical publications are available through the international open-access repository Zenodo, making her work accessible to researchers, artists and the global academic community.

Nona Dronova's artworks are presented on leading international art platforms, including Saatchi Art, Artmajeur, Zatista and Fine Art America, where they are available to collectors, designers and art enthusiasts worldwide.

Today, Nona Dronova Art Studio serves as the centre of her multidisciplinary artistic practice and digital archive. Conceived as a living museum collection rather than a traditional portfolio, it brings together paintings, decorative art, textile works, jewellery design, interior concepts, theoretical publications and artistic research within a single creative space. The archive documents the evolution of the artist's work through curated collections, thematic series and research projects, reflecting a lifelong dialogue between fine art, design, science and nature.

Artistic Philosophy
“For me, painting is not simply the creation of an image. It is a visual language through which colour, texture and form reveal the emotional memory of nature. Every work begins with observation but gradually becomes an independent artistic reality, where painting, design and theory exist in a continuous dialogue. My goal is not to imitate nature, but to transform its rhythms, colours and inner harmony into a contemporary artistic language.”
— Nona Dronova
