Nona Dronova Art Studio

A Contemporary Artistic Universe Where Painting, Design and Theory Meet.

Nona Dronova Art Studio is a digital gallery presenting the artistic world of contemporary artist, researcher and art theorist Nona Dronova.

The collection brings together paintings, decorative panels, botanical abstraction, textile art, interior concepts, jewellery design and experimental mixed-media works, reflecting a distinctive artistic vision developed through many years of creative practice.

Working at the intersection of Post-Symbolism, Expressionism and Botanical Abstraction, Nona Dronova has developed her own visual language based on colour, rhythm, texture and the emotional transformation of nature.

Alongside her artistic practice, she publishes theoretical research on contemporary painting and artistic language, exploring the relationship between colour, composition and visual expression.

This website is conceived as a living digital archive where artworks, research and creative projects come together, inviting visitors to explore the evolution of the artist's work through curated collections and thematic series.
